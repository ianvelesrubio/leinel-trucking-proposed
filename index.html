Reliable & expert hauling services
for your construction needs.

LEI-NEL Trucking Services helps you streamline your construction project by ensuring timely deliveries, and keeping your site safe and efficient. With reliable equipment and experienced crews, you avoid costly delays, reduce labor burdens, and maintain a clean workspace—so you can focus on building.

Services we offer:

Backfilling

We specialize in professional backfilling services for construction companies, delivering precision and reliability to keep your projects on schedule.

Aggregates hauling

We deliver high-quality sand, gravel, crushed stone, and specialty aggregates to construction sites, contractors, and landscaping projects. We ensure your materials arrive on spec and on schedule—every time.

Waste disposal

We solve the #1 headache for contractors: delayed projects due to overflowing debris. We are problem-solvers, and we want you to focus on more important things. Let us take care of the rest.
